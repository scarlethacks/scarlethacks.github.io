Our Inclusion Goal

To promote the best possible environment for ScarletHacks, our goal is to reach a 50/50 admission rate for male and female hackers. Invite your friends to apply!

Frequently Asked Questions


What is a Hackathon?

A hackathon is like an "invention marathon." Participants team up with other students and create something new in 36 hours! There will be free food, lots of bright students, and companies from around the midwest to help you learn more about technology and have a great time.

Can I participate?

Any university students (not just CS/EE/IT) above the age of 18 can attend ScarletHacks. Encourage your friends and classmates to apply!
